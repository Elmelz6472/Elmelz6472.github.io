Welcome to our Real-time Kicky-Ups Tracking App!

This app uses your webcam to track and analyze your body movements while doing kicky-ups in real-time.

Start by enabling your webcam and try doing some kicky-ups. The app will identify the body part you're using and keep track of your performance.
